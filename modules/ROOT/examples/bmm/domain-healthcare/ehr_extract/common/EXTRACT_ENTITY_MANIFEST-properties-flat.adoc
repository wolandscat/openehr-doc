3+^h|  *class EXTRACT_ENTITY_MANIFEST*


h|
2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*extract_id_key*: xref:representation:builtin_types-gen.adoc#_class_string[String]
a|Identifier by which this entity is known in the Extract. May be one of the other
identifiers, e.g. ehr_id or subject_id, or it may be something else, including a simple
integer.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*ehr_id*: xref:representation:identification-gen.adoc#_class_hier_object_id[HIER_OBJECT_ID]
a|EHR / EMR identifier for the entity at the target system.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*subject_id*: xref:representation:identification-gen.adoc#_class_party_ref[PARTY_REF]
a|Subject (i.e. patient or similar) identifier for the entity at the target system.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*other_ids*: xref:representation:builtin_types-gen.adoc#_class_list[List]<xref:representation:identification-gen.adoc#_class_object_ref[OBJECT_REF]>
a|Other identifiers that may be used to find the entity at the target system, keyed by type.
May include medicare numbers, drivers license number, tax number etc.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*item_list*: xref:representation:builtin_types-gen.adoc#_class_list[List]<xref:representation:identification-gen.adoc#_class_object_ref[OBJECT_REF]>
a|List of Uids of items to be included in the Extract, in cases where individual items are
being requested by id. More typically, this attribute is not used, and the
`EXTRACT_SPEC._criteria query` defines the Extract contents. If set, for openEHR data,
these are Uids identifying the version containers.
