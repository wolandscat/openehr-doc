3+^h|  *class MESSAGE*

h|*Description*
2+a|An authored, possibly signed, piece of content intended for one or more recipients.
Since the recipient may or may not be known directly, recipients are specified in
the ADDRESSED_MESSAGE class.

h|
2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*author*: xref:representation:representation-data_marking.adoc#_class_party_proxy[PARTY_PROXY]
a|Party responsible for the message content, who may or may not be technically
responsible for its creation.

h|prop +
  
|*audit*: xref:infostructure:infostructure-versioning.adoc#_class_audit_details[AUDIT_DETAILS]
a|Details of who actually created the message and when.

h|prop +
  
|*content*: xref:domain:domain-healthcare-ehr_extract.adoc#_class_message_content[MESSAGE_CONTENT]
a|Content of the message.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*signature*: xref:representation:representation-builtin_types.adoc#_class_string[String]
a|Optional signature by the author of message content in openPGP format.
