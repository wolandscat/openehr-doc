2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*description*: xref:representation:data_structures-gen.adoc#_class_item_structure[ITEM_STRUCTURE]
a|Description of the activity, in the form of an archetyped structure.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*timing*: xref:representation:data_types-gen.adoc#_class_dv_parsable[DV_PARSABLE]
a|Timing of the activity, in the form of a parsable string. If used, the preferred syntax is
ISO8601 'R' format, but other formats may be used including HL7 GTS.

May be omitted if:

* timing is represented structurally in the `description` attribute (e.g. via archetyped
elements), or
* unavailable, e.g. imported legacy data; in such cases, the `INSTRUCTION._narrative`
should carry text that indicates the timing of its `activities`.

h|prop +
  
|*action_archetype_id*: xref:representation:builtin_types-gen.adoc#_class_string[String]
a|Perl-compliant regular expression pattern, enclosed in '//' delimiters, indicating the
valid identifiers of archetypes for Actions corresponding to this Activity specification.

Defaults to `/.*/`, meaning any archetype.
