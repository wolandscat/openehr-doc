3+^h|  *class ADMIN_ENTRY*

h|*Description*
2+a|Entry subtype for administrative information, i.e. information about setting up the
clinical process, but not itself clinically relevant. Archetypes will define contained
information. Used for administrative details of admission, episode, ward location,
discharge, appointment. Not to be used for any clinically significant information.

h|*Inherit*
2+|xref:foundation:foundation-subject_record.adoc#_class_entry[ENTRY]

h|
2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*data*: xref:representation:representation-data_structures.adoc#_class_item_structure[ITEM_STRUCTURE]
a|Content of the Admin Entry. The data of the Entry; modelled in archetypes.

h|prop +
  
|*subject*: xref:representation:representation-data_marking.adoc#_class_party_proxy[PARTY_PROXY]
a|Id of human subject of this `ENTRY`, e.g.:

* organ donor
* foetus
* a family member
* another clinically relevant person.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*provider*: xref:representation:representation-data_marking.adoc#_class_party_proxy[PARTY_PROXY]
a|Optional identification of provider of the information in this `ENTRY`, which might be:

* the patient
* a patient agent, e.g. parent, guardian
* the clinician
* a device or software

Generally only used when the recorder needs to make it explicit. Otherwise, Composition
composer and other participants are assumed.
