3+^h|  *class INSTRUCTION*

h|*Description*
2+a|Used to specify actions in the future. Enables simple and complex specifications to
be expressed, including in a fully-computable workflow form. Used for any actionable
statement such as medication and therapeutic orders, monitoring, recall and review.
