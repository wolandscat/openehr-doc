3+^h|  *class INSTRUCTION*

h|*Description*
2+a|Used to specify actions in the future. Enables simple and complex specifications to be
expressed, including in a fully-computable workflow form. Used for any actionable
statement such as medication and therapeutic orders, monitoring, recall and review. Enough
details must be provided for the specification to be directly executed by an actor, either
human or machine.

Not to be used for plan items which are only specified in general terms.
