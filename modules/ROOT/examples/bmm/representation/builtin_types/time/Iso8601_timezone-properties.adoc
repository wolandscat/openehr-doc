3+^h|  *class Iso8601_timezone*

h|*Description*
2+a|ISO8601 timezone string, in format:

* `Z \| ±hh[mm]`

where:

* `hh` is "00" - "23" (0-filled to two digits)
* `mm` is "00" - "59" (0-filled to two digits)
* `Z` is a literal meaning UTC (modern replacement for GMT), i.e. timezone `+0000`

h|*Inherit*
2+|xref:representation:representation-builtin_types.adoc#_class_iso8601_type[Iso8601_type]

h|*Invariants*
2+a|

h|
2+a|min_hour_valid: sign = -1 ⇒ hour > 0 ∧ hour ≤ Min_timezone_hour;

h|
2+a|max_hour_valid: sign = 1 ⇒ hour > 0 ∧ hour ≤ Max_timezone_hour;

h|
2+a|minute_valid: ¬ minute_unknown ⇒ valid_minute(minute);

h|
2+a|sign_valid: sign = 1 ∨ sign = -1;
