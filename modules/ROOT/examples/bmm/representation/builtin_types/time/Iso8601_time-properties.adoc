3+^h|  *class Iso8601_time*

h|*Description*
2+a|Represents an ISO 8601 time, including partial and extended forms. Value may be
hh:mm:ss (extended, preferred), hhmmss (compact), or a partial invariant.

h|*Inherit*
2+|xref:representation:representation-builtin_types.adoc#_class_iso8601_type[Iso8601_type]

h|*Invariants*
2+a|

h|
2+a|hour_valid: valid_hour(hour, minute, second);

h|
2+a|minute_valid: ¬ minute_unknown ⇒ valid_minute(minute);

h|
2+a|second_valid: ¬ second_unknown ⇒ valid_second(second);

h|
2+a|fractional_second_valid: has_fractional_second ⇒ (¬ second_unknown ∧ valid_fractional_second(fractional_second));

h|
2+a|partial_validity: minute_unknown ⇒ second_unknown;
