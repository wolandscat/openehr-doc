3+^h|  *class Iso8601_date_time*

h|*Description*
2+a|Represents an ISO 8601 date/time, including partial and extended forms. Value may be:

* `YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss[(,\|.)sss][Z \| ±hh[:mm]]` (extended, preferred) or
* `YYYYMMDDThhmmss[(,\|.)sss][Z \|; ±hh[mm]]` (compact)
* or a partial variant.

See `valid_iso8601_date_time()` for validity.

Note that this class includes 2 deviations from ISO 8601:2004:

* for partial date/times, any part of the date/time up to the month may be missing, not
just seconds and minutes as in the standard;
* the time `24:00:00` is not allowed, since it would mean the date was really on the next
day.
