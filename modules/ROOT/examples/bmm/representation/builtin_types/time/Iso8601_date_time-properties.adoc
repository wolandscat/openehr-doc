3+^h|  *class Iso8601_date_time*

h|*Description*
2+a|Represents an ISO 8601 date/time, including partial and extended forms. Value may
be YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss (extended, preferred) or compact, or partial variants.

h|*Inherit*
2+|xref:representation:representation-builtin_types.adoc#_class_iso8601_type[Iso8601_type]

h|*Invariants*
2+a|

h|
2+a|year_valid: valid_year(year);

h|
2+a|month_valid: valid_month(month);

h|
2+a|day_valid: valid_day(year, month, day);

h|
2+a|hour_valid: valid_hour(hour, minute, second);

h|
2+a|minute_valid: ¬ minute_unknown ⇒ valid_minute(minute);

h|
2+a|second_valid: ¬ second_unknown ⇒ valid_second(second);

h|
2+a|fractional_second_valid: has_fractional_second ⇒ (¬ second_unknown ∧ valid_fractional_second(fractional_second));

h|
2+a|partial_validity_year: ¬ month_unknown;

h|
2+a|partial_validity_month: ¬ month_unknown;

h|
2+a|partial_validity_day: ¬ day_unknown;

h|
2+a|partial_validity_hour: ¬ hour_unknown;

h|
2+a|partial_validity_minute: minute_unknown ⇒ second_unknown;
