3+^h|  *class Iso8601_time*

h|*Description*
2+a|Represents an ISO 8601 time, including partial and extended forms. Value may be:

* `hh:mm:ss[(,\|.)sss][Z\|±hh[:mm]]` (extended, preferred) or
* `hhmmss[(,\|.)sss][Z\|±hh[mm]]` (compact)
* or a partial invariant.

See `valid_iso8601_time()` for validity.

NOTE: A small deviation to the ISO 8601:2004 standard in this class is that the time
`24:00:00` is not allowed, for consistency with `Iso8601_date_time`.
