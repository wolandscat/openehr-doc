3+^h|  *class LINK*

h|*Description*
2+a|Defines a logical relationship between two items, such as two ENTRYs or an ENTRY and a COMPOSITION.

h|
2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*meaning*: xref:representation:representation-data_types.adoc#_class_dv_text[DV_TEXT]
a|Used to describe the relationship, usually in clinical terms, such as in response to (the
relationship between test results and an order), follow-up to and so on. Such
relationships can represent any clinically meaningful connection between pieces of
information. Values for meaning include those described in Annex C, ENV 13606 pt 2 under
the categories of generic , documenting and reporting , organisational , clinical ,
circumstancial , and view management .

h|prop +
  
|*type*: xref:representation:representation-data_types.adoc#_class_dv_text[DV_TEXT]
a|The type attribute is used to indicate a clinical or domain-level meaning for the kind of
link, for example problem or issue . If type values are designed appropriately, they can
be used by the requestor of EHR extracts to categorise links which must be followed and
which can be broken when the extract is created.

h|prop +
  
|*target*: xref:representation:representation-data_types.adoc#_class_dv_ehr_uri[DV_EHR_URI]
a|The logical to object in the link relation, as per the linguistic sense of the meaning
attribute.
