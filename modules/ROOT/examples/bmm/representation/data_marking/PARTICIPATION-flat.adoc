3+^h|  *class PARTICIPATION*

h|*Description*
2+a|Model of a participation of a Party (any Actor or Role) in an activity. Used to represent
any participation of a Party in some activity, which is not explicitly in the model, e.g.
assisting nurse. Can be used to record past or future participations.

Should not be used in place of more permanent relationships between demographic entities.

h|
2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*function*: xref:representation:representation-data_types.adoc#_class_dv_text[DV_TEXT]
a|Function of performer in participation, e.g. 'resuscitation/CPR' in emergency situation

h|prop +
  0..1
|*mode*: xref:representation:representation-data_types.adoc#_class_dv_coded_text[DV_CODED_TEXT]
a|Optional field for recording the 'mode' of the performer / activity interaction,
e.g. present, by telephone, by email etc.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*time*: xref:representation:representation-data_types.adoc#_class_dv_interval[DV_INTERVAL]<xref:representation:representation-data_types.adoc#_class_dv_date_time[DV_DATE_TIME]>
a|The time interval during which the participation took place, if it is used in an
observational context (i.e. recording facts about the past); or the intended time
interval of the participation when used in future contexts, such as EHR Instructions.

h|prop +
  
|*performer*: xref:representation:representation-data_marking.adoc#_class_party_proxy[PARTY_PROXY]
a|The id and possibly demographic system link of the party participating in the activity.
