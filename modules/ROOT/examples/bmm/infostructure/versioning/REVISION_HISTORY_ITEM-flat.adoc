3+^h|  *class REVISION_HISTORY_ITEM*

h|*Description*
2+a|An entry in a revision history, corresponding to a version from a versioned container.
Consists of `AUDIT_DETAILS` instances with revision identifier of the revision to which
the `AUDIT_DETAILS` instance belongs.

h|*Inherit*
2+|xref:representation:builtin_types-gen.adoc#_class_any[Any]

h|
2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*version_id*: xref:representation:identification-gen.adoc#_class_object_version_id[OBJECT_VERSION_ID]
a|Version identifier for this revision.

h|prop +
  
|*audits*: xref:representation:builtin_types-gen.adoc#_class_list[List]<xref:infostructure:versioning-gen.adoc#_class_audit_details[AUDIT_DETAILS]>
a|The audits for this revision; there will always be at least one commit audit (which may
itself be an `ATTESTATION`), there may also be further attestations.
