3+^h|  *class BMM_ASSERTION*

h|*Description*
2+a|A statement that asserts the truth of its expression. If the expression evaluates to False
the execution generates an exception (depending on run-time settings).

May be rendered in syntax as `assert condition` or similar.

h|*Inherit*
2+|xref:languages:bmm-gen.adoc#_class_bmm_simple_statement[BMM_SIMPLE_STATEMENT]

h|
2+^h|*Feature Group "access"*

h|prop +
  
|*expression*: xref:languages:bmm-gen.adoc#_class_el_boolean_expression[EL_BOOLEAN_EXPRESSION]
a|Boolean-valued expression of the assertion.

h|prop +
  0..1
|*tag*: xref:representation:builtin_types-gen.adoc#_class_string[String]
a|Optional tag, typically used to designate design intention of the assertion, e.g.
`"Inv_all_members_valid"`.
